What Are AI Dashboard Reports?

We collect news and sentiment data then feed as much of it as possible through an AI model trained to understand potential market outcomes in the short to midterm. The guy writing the code was kinda inspired by Ray Dalio maybe...

This is a work in progress and is not intended to be taken as financial advice. No system can fully account for an individual’s unique financial needs or predict market movements with 100% certainty. That’s what financial advisors are for.

This will be the only "AI-Driven" content on the site, designed to condense complex data into quick, digestible snippets.

We will actively try to prevent search engines from indexing this content (and any purly AI content in general), as our goal is not to chase rank or mislead users. This will hopefully be a tool, we don't want another SEO junk builder.
