About Beyond The Stonks

Beyond The Stonks is run by two regular guys trying to figure out the world of finance, with no credentials, just curiosity and a dream of maybe, one day, hitting millionaire status.

We are not financial experts (and definitely not your financial advisors). What we are is meme-fueled, market-obsessed, and here to have fun with weird trades, market breakdowns, and the occasional crypto rabbit hole, mixed with a healthy dose of skepticism.

Follow along as we try to make sense of the chaos, laugh at AI bots and “get rich quick” bros, and learn what not to do in the process.
